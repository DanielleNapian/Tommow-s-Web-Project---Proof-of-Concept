About Danielle Napian

Danielle Napian

Danielle Napian is a self-taught artist, learning through youtube tutorials from other artists and adopting those skills to improve his ability. He loved making up characters and worlds as a kid. Facinated by anime, manga, character design, animation and inspired by artists, he began pursuing art more seriously.

Being a novice illustrator, he always dreamt to be an illustrator, concept artist for video game companies and be an animator for a movie or tv-series like arcane. He admires other artists with their skills and makes it as motivation to be a better illustrator.
